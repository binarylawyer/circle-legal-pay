The Problem We Solve

The $1 trillion legal industry is locked out of the Web3 economy because paying with crypto triggers complex ethical rules (NYSBA Rule 1.8(a)) due to volatility.

Our Solution

We remove volatility from the entire payment stack:

  • USDC Payment: 1:1 USD-backed stablecoin
  • USDC Gas: Arc uses USDC as native gas token
  • Circle Paymaster: Gasless client experience
  • AI Automation: Deterministic legal document generation
Ethical Compliance by Design

Because 100% of the transaction is dollar-denominated (payment + gas), we eliminate the "differing interest" that triggers Rule 1.8(a).

Result: The first compliant on-chain payment rail for legal services.

How It Works (A-to-Z Flow)

  1. Order: Client submits form or uses voice ("I need a Wyoming DAO")
  2. Payment: Client pays in USDC, funds move to escrow (gasless via Paymaster)
  3. Review: Human lawyer reviews AI-generated draft
  4. Approve: Lawyer approves with voice or click
  5. Document: AI generates deterministic legal document from template
  6. Deliver: Document uploaded to secure client locker
  7. Client Review: Client reviews and approves final document
  8. Payment Release: USDC released from escrow to law firm (on Arc)
  9. Recurring: Annual fees scheduled automatically (if applicable)
  10. Complete: Case finalized, document is yours
