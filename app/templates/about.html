About Agent-Ledger

Built for the "AI Agents on Arc with USDC" Hackathon

Agent-Ledger is a proof-of-concept platform demonstrating how AI agents, stablecoins, and blockchain technology can transform legal services.

Technology Stack

  • Arc Blockchain: USDC as native gas token
  • Circle WaaS: Developer-controlled wallets + Gas Station
  • ElevenLabs: Speech-to-Text for voice interface
  • Google Gemini: AI-powered intent extraction
  • Python Flask: Backend framework
  • SQLAlchemy: Database ORM
