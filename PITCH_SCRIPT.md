## **Delivery Tips**

1. **Practice Obsessively:** Run the demo 10+ times. Know every click.
2. **Time Yourself:** Use a timer. Stay under 3 minutes.
3. **Prepare for Failure:** Have backup screenshots if live demo fails.
4. **Energy & Confidence:** You're solving a billion-dollar problem. Own it.
5. **Know Your Judges:** If you know who's judging, tailor language to their background (technical vs. business).
